# UpStage Touch Screen Cheatsheet

How to perform on UpStage with your fingers (tablets / touch screens).

**The big rule everywhere: tap first to select, then make your gesture.**
Dragging no longer refreshes the page.

## Put things on the stage

| Gesture | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Double-tap a tile** *(easiest)* | Double-tap an avatar, prop, or stream tile in the toolbar panel. It appears in the **middle of the stage**, ready to move. |
| **Hold, then drag** *(hold ½ second)* | Press a tile and **hold still**. A green outline means "keep holding" — once the tile lifts, drag it anywhere on the stage and let go. Small finger wobbles are fine. |

## Move, resize, rotate

| Gesture | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Tap to select** | Tap an object once — the green frame with handles appears. Tap an empty part of the stage to put the frame away. |
| **Then drag it** | With the frame showing, drag the object with one finger. The **page stays still** — only the object moves. |
| **Corners resize** | Drag a corner or edge handle to resize, or the top handle to rotate. Handles are bigger on touch screens. |

> **Avatars are special:** you must hold an avatar before you can move it.
> **Double-tap the avatar** (on stage or in the Depth list) to claim it — you'll see the red teardrop — then tap-and-drag as usual.

## Menus

| Gesture | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Long-press = right-click** *(hold ½ second, don't move)* | Press and hold on any stage object without moving your finger. The context menu opens — flip, transparency, volume, bring to front, delete, and more. |
| **Active buttons glow green** | Toggle tiles (like the eraser) show a **green outline** when they're on. No outline = off. |

## Drawing

| Gesture | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Draw with a finger** | Object drawing and live whiteboard both work directly with your finger. |
| **Eraser on / off** | Tap **Erase** — it gets the green outline while erasing. Tap it again (or tap the size dot) to go back to the pen. The pen tile is outlined while you're drawing. |

## Streams & meeting rooms

| Gesture | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Place like anything else** | Your camera tile, stream keys, and meeting rooms all place with **double-tap** or hold-and-drag from the Streams panel. |
| **Meeting looks empty?** | Right after placing a meeting room, its frame is selected and the room inside is paused for input. **Tap an empty part of the stage once** — the meeting becomes clickable and you can join. |

## Good to know

- **The page won't pull-to-refresh anymore.** Swiping down while placing or moving something no longer reloads UpStage.
- Two-finger pinch still zooms the whole page if you need a closer look.
- If something refuses to move, check first that it's **selected** (green frame) — and for avatars, that **you** hold them (red teardrop).
